FAQ

What are the safety measures in place to protect visitors and animals?
Our zoo is committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of both our visitors and animals. We have several safety measures in place, including barriers between animal enclosures and public walkways, clear signage for restricted areas, and trained staff members monitoring the exhibits. Additionally, our zoo adheres to strict hygiene protocols, and hand sanitizing stations are available throughout the park. We encourage visitors to follow posted guidelines, respect the animals' space, and report any concerns to our staff.
Are there any educational programs or guided tours available?
Yes, our zoo offers a variety of educational programs and guided tours designed for visitors of all ages. We provide daily animal talks, feeding demonstrations, and interactive experiences that allow visitors to learn more about the animals and their habitats. Our knowledgeable guides lead both public and private tours, sharing fascinating insights and fun facts about our diverse collection of species. For more information and to book a guided tour, please visit our website or contact our visitor services team.
Do you have any special events or seasonal activities at the zoo?
Our zoo hosts a range of special events and seasonal activities throughout the year, offering unique experiences for our visitors. Some popular events include our annual Earth Day celebration, Halloween-themed "Boo at the Zoo," and holiday lights display during the winter season. We also organize themed weekends, conservation awareness days, and family-friendly activities that provide engaging and educational experiences for all. Be sure to check our events calendar on our website for the latest updates and schedules.
Can I bring my own food and beverages into the zoo, or are there dining options available?
While we allow visitors to bring their own food and non-alcoholic beverages into the zoo, we also offer a variety of on-site dining options for your convenience. Our food courts and snack stands serve a wide range of food choices, including sandwiches, salads, burgers, and ice cream. We also have vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available to accommodate dietary preferences. Please note that glass containers, alcohol, and single-use plastic straws are not permitted in the zoo to ensure the safety of our animals and the environment.
What is the zoo's policy on accessibility and accommodations for visitors with disabilities?
Our zoo is dedicated to providing an inclusive and enjoyable experience for all visitors. We offer wheelchair and stroller rentals at our main entrance, and most pathways and facilities are wheelchair-accessible. Accessible parking spaces are available near the entrance, and service animals are welcome in most areas of the zoo. For visitors with hearing impairments, we provide sign language interpretation for select programs and events when requested in advance. If you have any specific accessibility needs or questions, please do not hesitate to contact our visitor services team for assistance.
