Jingle Jangle

Jingle Jangle is a celebration of the hip-hop genre, which has its roots in African American and Latino communities in the Bronx, New York City in the 1970s. The festival brings together fans of rap music from all over the world to enjoy performances from some of the biggest names in the genre. It's an opportunity to experience the music live, to see the artists perform their biggest hits and to discover new talent in the rap music scene. Rap music is not just about the music itself, but also about the culture that surrounds it, including dance, fashion, and visual art. Rap music festivals celebrate this culture, with performers and fans alike dressing up in bold and colorful outfits, dancing to the beat, and enjoying the energy and excitement of the event. Overall, a Rap music festival is an immersive and unforgettable experience that celebrates the power and creativity of hip-hop culture.

About Us

Jingle Jangle is a music festival that was founded in 2019, and it quickly became one of the most highly anticipated events of the year. The festival takes place in a large outdoor venue and features a diverse lineup of artists spanning multiple genres, from indie rock to hip-hop and electronic music. In addition to the music, Jingle Jangle also offers a range of food and beverage options, art installations, and other immersive experiences that make it a unique and memorable event. Despite only being around for a few years, Jingle Jangle has already built a dedicated following of music fans who look forward to it as a highlight of the festival season. With its commitment to showcasing new and emerging artists alongside established headliners, Jingle Jangle is helping to shape the future of music festivals and create a space for creativity and community to flourish.
